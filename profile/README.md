## Meistä

Kelovartijat eli Keva on 1961 perustettu Tapiolalainen/Espoolainen noin 70 ihmisen partiolippukunta. Kokoonnumme Tapiolan kirkon alakerrassa sijaitsevalla kololla.

## About us

Kelovartijat (also known as "Keva") is a local scouts group in Tapiola/Espoo Finland with about 70 members. We are located in a meeting place in the basement floor of Tapiola church.
