# Kelovartijat ry

Kelovartijat eli Keva on 1961 perustettu Tapiolalainen/Espoolainen noin 70 ihmisen partiolippukunta. Kokoonnumme Tapiolan kirkon alakerrassa sijaitsevalla kololla.
****
Kelovartijat (also known as "Keva") is a local scouts group in Tapiola/Espoo Finland wiht about 70 members. We are located in a meeting place in the basement floor of Tapiola church.
